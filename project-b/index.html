Flipping City

Without interaction, the city will start from lines and grow into a map with dense and crossed lines. Clicking at a certain point will plant a little flower there, which is adding to the greenery of the city.

Flip your hand up, it'll show the side view of the city, with buildings growing from the ground. Maybe this reminds you of Hong Kong, a city that you cannot see the sky, especially near the Victoria Harbor. However, here, you can have the chance to move the buildings away and see the blue sky above.

Flip your hand down, get back to the overview of the city.

I want to send the message about city extension and the importance of including nature into a city.
