Family:

Flytraps in the jungle.

Cyberspace.

Appearance:

A flower-like creature living in constant floating codes.

Key Characteristics:

  • It interacts with the player as the mouse clicks and approaches, showing different appearance and movements.
  • It tempts the user to come closer, swallow the mouse as food, and reproduce more.
  • Waiting for further discovery...

What we know so far about digital mousetrap?

It maintains a calm appearance without interaction. But any approach and clicks from the mouse will lead to a sudden change of the mousetrap. It'll take in the mouse as energy and reproduce, acting as fast travelling bacteria.


Guidelines for human interaction with this creature:

Approach its center, then it’ll change its appearance.

Click the mouse, then it’ll start changing color.

Maintain in its core for sometime, then the mousetrap will start reproducing in the surrounding locations.
